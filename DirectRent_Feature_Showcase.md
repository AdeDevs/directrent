## 🔒 Slide 2: The Core Problem vs. DirectRent Solution
* **The Problem (Nigerian Housing Pain Points):**
  1. **Identity & Trust Scams:** Fake property dealers posting non-existent houses or double-leasing single listings.
  2. **Mediator Fees:** Heavy extortionate agency and agreement fees (often up to 30% of annual rent).
  3. **Payment Flexibility:** Bulky, locked annual rents with no representation or safety margin for students.
* **The DirectRent Solution:**
  1. **Immutable NIN Enforcement:** Every agent and tenant has verified 11-digit verified IDs which become permanent once uploaded.
  2. **Direct, Transparent Interface:** Zero arbitrary intermediary fees.
  3. **Co-living & Flexible Terms:** Filters for monthly, quarterly, and co-shared student listings.

---

## 🛡️ Slide 3: Interactive Visual Trust Suite
* **Slide Title:** Immutable National Verification Engine
* **Headline:** Establishing Real Peer-to-Peer Safety
* **Key Features to Highlight:**
  * **11-Digit National Identification Number (NIN):** Secure, immutable identification pipeline. Once submitted, NIN inputs lock instantly (read-only) and display a verified secure shield.
  * **Phone OTP Verification:** SMS visual OTP loops to guarantee that active communication numbers cannot be spoofed.
  * **Dynamically Calculated Verification Scorecards:** 
    * `None` (Unverified/Base Account)
    * `Trusted` (Phone or NIN verified)
    * `Verified` (Complete double-verification check active)
* **Slide Visual Suggestion:** Before/After visual showing input panels transforming into sleek lock boxes once submitted.

---

## 🏦 Slide 4: Unified Financial Wallet & Escrow Safety Lock
* **Slide Title:** Escrow Wallet Protocol
* **Headline:** Bulletproof Payment Assurances
* **Key Mechanisms:**
  * **Integrated Tenant Ledger:** Simple deposit, rapid transfer, and withdrawal interface, secured with customizable visual 4-digit Wallet PIN gates.
  * **Escrow Rent Locking:** When rent is paid, funds are locked in independent, secure platform custody.
  * **Visual State Machine:** Transactions map cleanly through:
    * `Locked` (Awaiting visual house tours or keys handover)
    * `Released` (Key successfully given — host gets paid)
    * `Refunded` (Listing mismatches or host cancellations)
    * `Disputed` (Administrative mediation triggers)
  * **Zero Loose Cash:** Simple bank connection slots and immediate receipt downloads for students to showcase proof of payments.
